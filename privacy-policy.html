Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 14, 2025

XpenseTrac values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

XpenseTrac does not collect, store, or share any personal or financial information. All data you enter into the app—such as expenses, budgets, or notes—is stored only on your device.

2. Data Storage

Your information is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. It remains entirely within your control on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

XpenseTrac does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

4. Security

Since your data stays on your device, your privacy and security depend on your device’s settings and operating system. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with a passcode.
